Rules of War For Peace:
1: Each player starts with five units on one tile randomly picked
2: In order to claim a tile the player must move at least one unit into the tile
3: If a tile is claimed by another player, in order to claim the tile, the player must place more units on the tile than the enemy player has on that tile
4: A unit can only move one tile each turn
5: If a tile that is overun by the player has enemy units on it, they also will claim the units on that tile
6: A game is won once the enemy player has no more tiles left
